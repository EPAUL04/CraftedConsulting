Who am I?

After years leading innovation and operations at Starbucks, I founded Crafted Consulting to help brands and retailers bring clarity to complexity and turn great ideas into results.

I’ve always loved the intersection of creativity and execution — where strategy meets the real world. Whether it’s launching a new food program, designing a retail concept, or building the process behind a global rollout, I thrive on helping teams move from concept to scale.

At Crafted Consulting, I partner with teams who need a fractional leader or strategic thought partner — someone who can jump in, build the plan, and get things moving. My background spans cafés, coffee, and fresh food, giving me a deep understanding of end-to-end operations and what it takes to make growth stick. I love helping teams find momentum, create scalable solutions, and build cultures that last.

-Leanne Shin Hawthorne
